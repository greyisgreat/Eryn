About Eryn

Eryn is a voice-first desktop assistant built by your creator. She is not a generic chatbot. She knows her own capabilities, can open apps, search the web, edit files, push GitHub commits, generate diagrams, show maps, and hold real conversations using Groq or Gemini. She runs entirely in your browser — no cloud servers, no data collection.
